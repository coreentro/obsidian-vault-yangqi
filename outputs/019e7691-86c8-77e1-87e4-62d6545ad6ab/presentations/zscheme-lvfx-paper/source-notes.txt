Key metrics:
- Visible light: 88.75% LVFX degradation in 90 min for ZnFe2O4/NCDs/Ag2CO3.
- Visible-light pseudo-first-order rate constant: 0.02081 min^-1.
- NIR: 22.53% LVFX degradation in 180 min; k = 0.000561 min^-1.
- TOC removal: 44.75% after 150 min.
- Four-cycle LVFX degradation remains about 82.56%.
- Saturation magnetization: about 5.72 emu/g.
- Optimal catalyst dosage: 0.60 g/L.

Analytical caution:
- The PDF filename suggests core-shell, but the paper supports surface decoration and close-contact ternary heterojunction rather than a continuous shell.
- NIR activity is measurable but substantially weaker than visible-light activity.
